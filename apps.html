My Final Project
Air Hockey

For our final project, my group members and I decided to create an air hockey game. We used the knowledge we learned in CSC106 this semester to
put together a fun game. We used variables, loops, functions, and logic to create this final project. In order to play the game, press the
customization button to pick a team, head back to the home screen and press start. Use the mouse to hit the puck into the net with the paddle to
score points! Careful: If your opponent (the Black Badgers) takes the lead, the audience will no longer cheer for you!
